About

PhD candidate in pharmacoepidemiology at the Azrieli Research Centre, CHU Sainte-Justine, supervised by Prof. Anick Bérard (Canada Research Chair, Tier 1). My doctoral research uses causal inference methods — primarily interrupted time series — to evaluate the impact of risk management plans on prenatal exposure to fetotoxic medications, leveraging the Quebec Pregnancy Cohort (~559K pregnancies, 1998–2022).

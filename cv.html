Summary

PhD candidate specializing in pharmacoepidemiology and perinatal drug safety at the Azrieli Research Centre, CHU Sainte-Justine, supervised by Prof. Anick Bérard (Canada Research Chair Tier 1). Doctoral research uses causal inference methods — primarily interrupted time series — applied to the Quebec Pregnancy Cohort (~559K pregnancies, 1998–2022) to evaluate whether risk management plans reduce prenatal exposure to fetotoxic medications. Actively targeting industry roles in pharmacovigilance, drug safety, RWE, HEOR, and medical affairs.

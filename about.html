About

Researcher. Epidemiologist. Future industry scientist.

I'm a PhD Candidate in Pharmaceutical Sciences at Université de Montréal, based at the Azrieli Research Centre, CHU Sainte-Justine, supervised by Prof. Anick Bérard (Canada Research Chair, Tier 1). My doctoral research evaluates the real-world impact of risk management plans on prenatal exposure to teratogenic and fetotoxic medications, using 25 years of data from the Quebec Pregnancy Cohort.

I am particularly interested in roles in pharmacovigilance, drug safety, RWE, and HEOR — fields where rigorous evidence shapes regulatory, clinical, and strategic decisions. I also hold hands-on observership experience in pharmacovigilance (CRPV Cochin, AP-HP, Paris) and OB-GYN (Hôpital Pitié-Salpêtrière, Paris).
